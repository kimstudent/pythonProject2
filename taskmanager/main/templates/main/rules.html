Rules

General Rules

Follow these general rules to maintain optimal performance of your smart home system:

  • Update your devices to the latest firmware version.
  • Ensure your network connection is secure and stable.
  • Regularly check and replace batteries in wireless devices.
  • Create backups of your device configurations.
  • Check the operation of your smart home system at least once a month.

Safety Rules

Safety is a priority. Follow these safety rules:

  • Do not overload sockets with too many devices.
  • Ensure that all electrical installations are carried out by professionals.
  • Regularly check smoke detectors and fire alarms.
  • Install a video surveillance system for added security.
  • Use only certified devices and components.

Privacy Rules

Respect privacy and follow these guidelines:

  • Use strong, unique passwords for all devices and accounts.
  • Regularly check and manage permissions for all connected devices.
  • Be aware of and comply with local privacy laws and regulations.
  • Do not share personal data with third parties without necessity.
  • Use encryption to protect data transmitted over the network.

Operating Tips

For optimal performance of your smart home system:

  • Keep track of software updates and install them promptly.
  • Use automation to simplify daily tasks.
  • Regularly test the operation of sensors and actuators.
  • Create scenarios for automating processes in your smart home.
  • Integrate your smart home with other systems to increase convenience and functionality.
